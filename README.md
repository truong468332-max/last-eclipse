# Last Eclipse 🖤⚔️

**Last Eclipse** - A dark, fast-paced pixel action game inspired by Katana Zero with deep storytelling, moral choices, and multiple endings.

## 🌑 Story

**Year 2189**

A mysterious event called **The Black Rain** engulfed the world. After the rain:
- Animals mutated
- Humans became radioactive
- Demon-like creatures emerged
- Cities turned into dead zones

### The Truth

Demon-like creatures are actually **Mutated Humans and Animals** - victims of radiation, viruses, and biological toxins.

You play as **Kai**, a demon hunter who discovers he can:
- Absorb demon powers
- See their memories
- Understand the truth behind the apocalypse

### The Real Enemy

It's not demons vs humans.

Everyone is a victim.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

### Core Mechanics (Katana Zero Style)
- **One-hit-one-kill** - Kai dies in 1 hit, demons die in 1 hit
- **Puzzle-like combat** - Each level is a timing/strategy puzzle
- **Demon Absorption** - Defeat demons to gain their powers
- **Memory System** - Absorb memories to see the truth

### Player Abilities
- **Dash** - Quick dodge movement
- **Absorb** - Consume defeated demons
- **Memory Vision** - See demon memories for clues
- **Power Absorption** - Use demon abilities

## 🏛️ Factions

### Iron Dominion ⚔️
**Military extremists**
- Goal: Eliminate all demons, control the world
- Weapons: Advanced military tech
- Threat: Authoritarian regime

### Eden Union 🧬
**Scientists**
- Belief: Can cure the infection
- Tools: Biotech, medicine
- Hope: Restoration

### Ash Walkers 🏜️
**Desert survivors**
- Hunt demons for resources
- Like mercenaries
- Morally gray

### Children of Void 🌀
**Mysterious cult**
- Belief: Apocalypse is evolution
- Goal: Complete world destruction
- Ideology: Nihilism

---

## 📖 Story & Endings

### Plot Twists

**Twist 1:** The Black Rain wasn't a natural disaster - it was created by a pre-war organization

**Twist 2:** Kai is part of that organization

**Twist 3:** The world has been destroyed and reborn many times - humanity is in a cycle

### Three Endings

**Ending 1: Extermination**
- Destroy all demons
- Humanity survives but world continues decaying
- Empty victory

**Ending 2: Fusion**
- Merge with demons
- Create a new hybrid species
- Evolution or abomination?

**Ending 3: Restart**
- Destroy the source of the Black Rain
- Reset civilization
- But at what cost?

## 🖤 Design Philosophy

**Last Eclipse** is built on the principle that:

> "In a world where everyone is a victim, who is the real enemy?"

The game challenges players to question:
- What is right and wrong?
- Can enemies become allies?
- Is survival worth the cost?
- Can a broken world be fixed?
